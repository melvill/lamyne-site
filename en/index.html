La MYNE
Tiers-Lieu Open Source

A Place of Freedom, Experimentation
and Citizen Innovation

A place for Work and Prototyping

La M[Y]NE opens the doors of its house and welcomes you to its Serious Space for co-working, the Lab[O] and Atel[I]er for experiments of all kinds without forgetting the garden for your permaculture desires. Find us every Wednesday evening at the OX[Y]GEN sessions.

A peer-to-peer learning approach

La M[Y]NE offers all a framework for the development of a project by peer-to-peer learning based on research and experimentation. Members of La M[Y]NE can benefit freely from our workshops and masterclasses program.

Contribute to the project of LA MYNE

La M[Y]NE is a co-constructed space and co-managed by the community. Fed by an effort of transparency, we ensure open governance and economic model(s). Support La M[Y]NE project by becoming a member and / or participating in the work groups.
